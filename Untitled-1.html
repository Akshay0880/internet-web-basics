Mini Project Title : ASSIGNMENT TRACKER

Information: Easily set reminders for assignments and receive email notifications before deadlines.

  1. Problem Statement: Students often forget assignment deadlines due to a lack of a structured tracking system, which leads to missed submissions and poor academic performance.
  2. Objective: To develop a web-based platform that allows students to add, track, and receive reminders for upcoming assignments in an efficient and user-friendly manner.
  3. Goal: Ensure students never miss assignment deadlines by providing timely alerts and a centralized view of all tasks across subjects.
  4. Result/Output: A responsive web application where users can log in, add assignments with due dates and subjects, and receive visual and/or email reminders before deadlines.
  5. Technology Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Firebase (for authentication and database), and EmailJS or similar service for email alerts.
  6. User Audience: Primarily students in schools, colleges, or universities who want to manage their academic tasks and due dates efficiently.
  7. Impact on Society: The tool encourages better time management and organization among students, leading to improved academic performance and reduced stress.
